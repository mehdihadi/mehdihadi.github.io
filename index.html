About

I am an experienced environmental data scientist with over 10 years of experience in water quality and health, quantitative microbial risk assessment, environmental data and spatial data analysis. I am expert in using R for machine learning, spatial data analysis, environmental epidemiology, and web-based app development using R Shiny. I have around 4 years of experience in Shiny, and I have developed several web-based apps using this framework. These skills have enabled me to develop effective data visualization tools that can be used for public health decision-making. I plan to use my skills to analyze relationships between WASH variables and health effects and also to develop web applications for environmental health data visualization and dissemination. I enjoy problem-solving and coding.

  • Languages: R, VB, JavaScript, HTML/CSS
  • Databases: MySQL, SQLite, MongoDB
  • Frameworks: Shiny, Keras, TensorFlow
  • Tools & Technologies: Git, Docker

Looking for an opportunity to work in a challenging position combining my skills in environmetal data analysis, which provides professional development, interesting experiences and personal growth.
